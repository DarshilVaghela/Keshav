Our Story

About Us

Keshav was opened on Friendship Day 2000. Chef / Owner Raj Gohel began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and his neighbors out of a small kitchen at the corner of Jamnagar and Bhavnagar. Today, Jamnagar's beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 24 years of classic, made from scratch Indian cooking.
